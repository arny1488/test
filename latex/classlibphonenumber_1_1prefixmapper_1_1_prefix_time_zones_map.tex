lookup\+Time\+Zones\+For\+Number\+Key (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{}]{\$key}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})\hspace{0.3cm}{\ttfamily [protected]}}

Returns the list of time zones {\ttfamily key} corresponds to.

{\ttfamily key} could be the calling country code and the full significant number of a certain number, or it could be just a phone-\/number prefix. For example, the full number 16502530000 (from the phone-\/number +1 650 253 0000) is a valid input. Also, any of its prefixes, such as 16502, is also valid.
