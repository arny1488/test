\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Handles binary/7/8-\/bit Transfer Encoding in \doxylink{class_swift}{Swift} \doxylink{class_mailer}{Mailer}.

When sending 8-\/bit content over SMTP, you should use \doxylink{class_swift___transport___esmtp___eight_bit_mime_handler}{Swift\+\_\+\+Transport\+\_\+\+Esmtp\+\_\+\+Eight\+Bit\+Mime\+Handler} to enable the 8BITMIME SMTP extension.
